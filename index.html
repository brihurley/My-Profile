How it all began

My journey into The Performing Arts started when I was just 5 years old in Oliver Hurley's StageSchool.
When I was 8 I began attending Fame in Miltown where I got my first role as Gretal in The Sound Of Music
and made my singing debut with "Roar" by Katy Perry. After 2 years in Fame in 2016
Jackie O' Mahonys School of Performing Arts opened, and thats where I continued to stay until I turned 18,
with my last show being my overall best performance with
my most amount of roles in a singular show. My last 3 performances spanned over just 2 days
where I played Catherine Of Aragon, Henry the 8ths first wife in Six the musical,
One of the mudering wifes in Chicago, many extra parts and my singing moment in
crusing for a brusing from high school musical. I ended my 3rd and final show with my graduation on stage.

My Acting Career

I remember being very young and having a script in my hand and microphone in front of my face
both my parents had been working for Radio Kerry.
I was old enough to read to but still sounded very young, perfect for Radios Ads.
I loved it, sitting down with the headphones on and reading what had been typed out for me.
I appeared on the breakfast show with my dad
and then when he moved on from Radio I came with him.
Video advertisements were a bit scarier, the camera can be intimidating but
I quickly learned to just let all the nerves go before I began.
Then came the live shows, no editing could be done so I had to get it right the first time.
I have loved nothing more then the feeling of stepping out on stage
and getting to be someone else for a bit. No stress, no worries, just Singing, Dancing and Acting.
